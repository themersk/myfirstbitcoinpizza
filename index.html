On May 22, 2010, the first purchase was made using Bitcoin.

Laszlo Hanyecz purchased two pizzas for a grand total of 10,000 Bitcoins. (In today's money that's somewhere around $95 MILLION — but that's not really the point.) This exchange helped legitimize Bitcoin as a real-world currency. Today, the whole process is a whole lot easier — and much cheaper — with the help of the Lightning Network.

Step 1: Get some Bitcoin

Let's buy some Bitcoin! We recommend Cash App because its user friendly and widely available to everybody. Sync your bank account by scanning your debit card, and tap on the "Bitcoin" tab. You may be asked some personal information before you're approved to make the first purchase. Let's start with a small amount to get started. The average order on Lightning Pizza is around $25-30, so that's a good amount to start with for now.

Step 2: Transfer your funds

Now you have to transfer your Bitcoin to an external wallet. We like Bluewallet because it makes the next step much easier. In Bluewallet, create a new Bitcoin wallet. Open it up and tap "Recieve." You should see a long string of numbers. This is your address, and it tells people where to send your Bitcoin. Copy this address and hop back into Cash App. Select the "Withdraw" option, and paste your Bitcoin address. That's it! Now we have to wait for the transaction to be approved by the blockchain, which should take around 10 minutes.

Step 3: Set up your Lightning wallet

While you're waiting, create a new Lightning wallet withing Bluewallet. This allows you to make instant and cheap transactions over the Lightning Network. Lightning transactions make using Bitcoin for everyday payments simple and easy. Once your Bitcoin has been transferred to your Bitcoin wallet, you can move your funds to the Lightning wallet. Keep in mind: your Lightning wallet is intended for use for payments, not for storing your Bitcoin. Only store small amounts of bitcoin at a time in your lightning wallet.

Step 4: Buy some Pizza!

Hungry yet? Now comes the fun part! Tap the button below to order carryout or delivery from any Dominos in the US. Choose your order, and at the end you'll be presented with a QR code that you can scan with your Bluewallet to pay for the invoice, and experience the magic of Lightning! Bon appétit!
